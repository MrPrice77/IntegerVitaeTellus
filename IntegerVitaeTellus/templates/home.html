About

I love to cook and bake, but I've found that I have several cookbooks with only one or two recipes I care about. That is where the idea for Integer Vitae Tellus comes from. The title itself is nothing special, it just means cookbook in latin. The website itself however is beyond special. It allows you to post your favorite recipes so you can easily refer back to them rather than digging through piles of physical cookbooks. Soon, you will be able to create group cookbooks where you and your friends can easily share your recipes.
